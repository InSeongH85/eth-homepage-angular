About

GURI TOKEN's ultimate vision is to build a DAO to eliminate centralized power and to build an ecosystem that is transparent and reliable by providing a system for users to participate in project operations and decision-making, and to create an autonomous ecosystem where users can be rewarded fairly.

In addition, it will help users around the world familiarize themselves with the blockchain ecosystem through interesting content that combines K culture and Kmeme. Through this, we will promote blockchain participation through a cultural approach that provides both fun and value, and allow users to naturally participate in the DAO ecosystem.
